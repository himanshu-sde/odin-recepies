Plain Maggie Noodles

Description

Maggi 2-Minutes Noodles have been a classic Indian snack for a good few decades now. Nestle brings you another delicious instant food product - Maggi 2-Minute Masala Noodles!

  • Time - 10 minutes
  • Serves - 2 people

Ingredients

  • 2 packets Maggi noodles
  • 1/4 teaspoon Salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon Black-pepper powder
  • 5 Cup Water

    • Cooking Instructions

    Step 1

    Take water in the pan and bring it to boil.

    Step 2

    Add Maggi seasoning, salt,turmeric powder and black- pepper powder to it.

    Step 3

    Add Maggi noodles. Bring it to boil on high flame and then reduce the heat.

    Step 4

    Add tomato ketchup and allow it to cook on simmer for a couple of minutes.

    Step 5

    Now, serve the Maggi noodles.
