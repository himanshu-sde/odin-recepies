Vegtable Omlette

Description

Omelettes are an egg breakfast consisting of eggs that have been beaten, then cooked until set and folded over. American omelets are filled with cheese, veggies, and/or protein. French omelettes are just eggs and butter without a filling.

  • Time - 25 minutes
  • Serves - 3-4 people

Ingredients

  • 3 Eggs
  • 3 medium Potato, 1 medium tomato
  • 2-3 green chilli, 3 tbsp chopped Capsicum
  • 1 medium Onion
  • Salt
  • 2 tbsp Oil
  • 4 tsp Milk
  • 2 tsp Butter

    • Cooking Instructions

    Step 1

    In moderately heated pan add butter / oil, add onion + green chilli and sauté till pinkish, then add potatoes + salt and sauté for 4-5 minutes under medium flame.

    Step 2

    In a mixing bowl, break eggs, add milk, salt + turmeric and mix well and keep aside.

    Step 3

    Add capsicum and sauté for 3-4 minutes.And the veggies are ready.

    Step 4

    Now add the cooked veggie into egg mixture and add chopped tomatoes also and mix nicely.

    Step 5

    Now in a pan add butter, pour the mixture and spread evenly all around the pan and cook for 4-5 minutes by covering under low to medium flame.

    Step 6

    Now flip the other side of the omelette and cook for 3-4 minutes under low flame, switch off gas and keep in stand by for 1-2 minutes.

    Step 7

    Now omelette is cooked and allow to cool.

    Step 8

    Cut into pieces while it is warm and served with tomato sauce. It can be punched in between bread slices too.
