Masala Poha

Description

Masala Poha is a mild spicy and filling dish made by cooking poha with masala powders, tempered onions and veggies. Masala poha could be relished as snack or as a meal for breakfast / dinner. How to make Masala Poha is described in this recipe with step by step pictures.

  • Time - 10 minutes
  • Serves - 4 people

Ingredients

  • 1 Chopped onion
  • 1 Chopped tomato
  • 1 tsp Turmeric powder
  • 2 tbsp Oil
  • Spices- Garam Masala, Coriander Powder and Salt (As per taste)

    • Cooking Instructions

    Step 1

    Take a pan add some oil into it. Now add chopped onion and tomato.Mix it well.

    Step 2

    Add turmeric powder,salt,red chilli powder,coriander powder,garam masala into it and Mixed it well.

    Step 3

    Take a bowl and add poha into it and wash it well with water.

    Step 4

    Add poha into the Pan and mix it well Cook it for 2 minutes

    Step 5

    Masala Poha is ready!! Serve it now.
